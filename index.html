THE ARTIST

About Amira

Contemporary Abstract Expressionist

Amira Rahim is an internationally collected abstract expressionist painter known for her vibrant use of color and energetic compositions. Born in New Jersey and now based between the US and UAE, her work has been featured in major publications and acquired by collectors worldwide.

With a background in International Relations and self-taught artistic skills, Amira brings a unique perspective to her work. Her paintings celebrate the joy of color and creative freedom, often inspired by her travels and multicultural experiences.

Beyond creating art, Amira is passionate about empowering other artists through education. Her signature program, Better Than Art School™, has helped hundreds of emerging artists develop their unique styles and build sustainable creative businesses.
