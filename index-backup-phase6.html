THE ARTIST

About Amira

Amira Rahim is an internationally collected abstract expressionist whose work graces the collections of the Al Nahyan Royal Family of Abu Dhabi—the world's wealthiest royal family with a net worth of $305 billion. Named a "2024 Top Global Licensing Agent" by License Global magazine, she has established herself as a leading contemporary artist and creative entrepreneur.

Achieving six-figure sales by her second year as a professional artist, Amira's distinctive abstract paintings are now collected in over 30 countries. Her global licensing partnerships span four continents, while her educational programs have trained 2,000+ students with a remarkable 4.9-star rating.
