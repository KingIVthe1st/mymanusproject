THE ARTIST

Investment-Grade Artist

Amira Rahim is an internationally collected abstract expressionist whose work graces the collections of the Al Nahyan Royal Family of Abu Dhabi. Named a "2024 Top Global Licensing Agent" by License Global magazine, she has established herself as a leading contemporary artist in the global art market.

Achieving six-figure sales by her second year as a professional artist, Amira's distinctive abstract paintings are now collected in over 30 countries. Her global licensing partnerships span four continents, while her educational legacy has trained 2,000+ students with a remarkable 4.9-star rating.
