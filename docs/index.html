THE ARTIST

About the Artist

Amira Rahim traded a legal career for a paintbrush, inspired by The Art of Getting By. Her bold, abstract expressionist works, bursting with vibrant color, are celebrated worldwide—collected by the Royal Family of Abu Dhabi and licensed by Wayfair and Amazon. As the author of Paint to Prosper (2023, 4.8/5 stars on Amazon), Amira empowers artists to turn passion into profit through her transformative courses and creative mentorship.
